The App

Mystic Feathers the Product Line. Our app! How does it work?

Customers can 'connect' with other people and talk about your problems/help others with their problems. Secondly the app can create a personalized schedule and manages the goals you want to achieve in life. It also keeps track on how much time you spend on social media and censors everything that might upset you. Lastly, to spread some positivity, there will be a meme every morning on the app to brighten up your day.

Unique Value Proposition

The main message of Mystic Feathers is to create a safe and positive space for people who need to hide from the cruel big world we live in. It is very hard for people to find this place, due to social media and other outside factors. Mystic Feathers’ brand and products are made to help people feel safe and free, to make them feel positive about themselves and to give them opportunities to express their fantasies.
