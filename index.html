VISION

Our company’s brand “Mystic Feathers” has a vision; that in a world where people can’t always escape from reality, we believe that creating a safe space will help them, because only then these people will truly find their happiness and hide from this cruel big world.

VALUES

Meaning that we are focused on the safety of others, creating positivity and fantasy, and we stand for freedom. We believe that our brand looks as bright as the sun after a rainy day and you experience it as a fresh breath of air after being inside all day. Our brand name is based on ‘Mystic’, as fantasy usually involves a mystical base, the base we are trying to create with our brand. Moreover, ‘Feathers’ stands for flying over the clouds, and that is what we want our product to feel like.
